Pre-cooled propulsion

sabre_precooler.py

ε-NTU heat exchanger model across the Mach 0.5–5 envelope. ISA atmosphere, isentropic ram compression, LH₂ heat sink budget, frost deposition via vapour pressure driving force.

Design point (M5, 25km): 163 MW heat load, 95.8% effectiveness, 49.5 kg/s LH₂. Target: Frazer-Nash INVICTUS, Rolls-Royce HVX

Motorsport

Carbon-carbon brake thermal management
Motorsport thermal

f1_brake_thermal.py

Transient lumped-capacitance model of an F1 C-C brake disc. Jet impingement cooling, radiation, Arrhenius oxidation kinetics. Silverstone vs Monaco lap profiles.

F1 discs exceed 750°C air-oxidation threshold routinely — real operating window is 600–1200°C with N₂ purge. Monaco peaks ~470°C hotter than Silverstone.
Target: Cadillac F1, Williams, Ricardo

Spacecraft Systems

Orbital thermal · attitude determination & control
Orbital thermal

spacecraft_thermal.py

Multi-orbit lumped capacitance thermal model. Eclipse cycles, solar/albedo/ Earth-IR loads, coatings database (white/black/OSR/gold/Kapton/Al), heater sizing.

3U CubeSat at 5W: white paint runs colder (-28°C) than black paint (+1°C) — solar absorption dominates at low power.
Target: SSTL, Open Cosmos
View source →

2D truss FEM with global stiffness assembly, Euler buckling margins, and an iterative member-sizing optimisation loop.

NACA 2412, 60 panels: dCl/dα ≈ 0.108/deg vs thin-airfoil theory limit 0.110/deg.
